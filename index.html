About Me

Who am I?

I am a 3rd year BSc student studying Computer Science Engineering at the IUBAT - International University of business Agriculture and Technology. Beside my degree studies, I have worked on a range of briefs including branding, web design, user interface design, and other social media designs.

By working on a range of projects this has helped me to gain a deeper understanding of a good aspect that is needed for graphic design. This has given me the skills needed to work in this environment including having an in-depth knowledge in areas such as logo design, poster and banner designs, and digital designs .I have I have enough knowledge in software such as Photoshop, Adobe XD, Illustrator and Gravit. I have improved the quality of my work and my software skills gained experience in using After Effects, Premiere Pro and experienced some remote works for web development.
